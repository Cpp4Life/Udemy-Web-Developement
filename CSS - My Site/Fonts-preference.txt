About setting properties of border-style, margin, ...
- 1 value applies to 4 edges.
- 2 values apply to "top - bottom" and "left - right" respectively.
- 3 values apply to "top", "left - right" and "bottom" respectively.
- 4 values apply to "top", "right", "bottom" and "left" respectively. (Clockwise)
